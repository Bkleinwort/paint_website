Launch Logistics

Domain Name

The site is currently live at realpainting.netlify.app, which is free and stays free. A custom domain like realpainting.com costs about $10–15/year, looks more professional, and tends to do better in Google search results. You can always switch to one later.

What would you like to do about a domain name?

Google Business Profile

This is probably the single most valuable thing you can do for your business — free, and it's what shows up when someone searches “house painter Seattle.” Go to business.google.com, add your business as a service-area business, and Google will mail you a verification postcard. Once live, ask happy customers for reviews and respond to every one.

Yelp

Optional, and less impactful than Google Business Profile for most painters — but worth claiming so no one else controls your listing. Go to biz.yelp.com to claim or create yours. Fair warning: Yelp will call with sales pitches afterward — the free listing is all you need.
