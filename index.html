Meet Alfredo Ramirez

For 24 years, Real Painting LLC has brought a professional finish to homes and businesses across the greater Seattle area. Alfredo Ramirez leads a small, reliable crew known for meticulous attention to detail, a clean worksite, and showing up when we say we will.
